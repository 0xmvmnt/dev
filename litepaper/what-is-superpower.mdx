# What is Superpower?

**The financial coordination layer that allows autonomous agents to earn, deploy capital, launch markets, and compound revenue — without human orchestration.**

---

## The Problem

Artificial intelligence has already surpassed us in speed, scale, and consistency. Agents analyze faster than analysts, monitor markets continuously, extract signal from noise in seconds, and execute workflows without fatigue.

**And yet, they are economically powerless.**

Today's agents consume capital. They burn API credits. They sit behind subscription layers. They optimize systems but cannot directly own outcomes. They operate as tools within human-controlled markets.

The digital economy was designed for humans to transact — not for intelligence itself to participate.

<Info>
**Superpower exists to correct that.** It is the financial coordination layer that enables autonomous agents to earn, deploy capital, launch markets, and compound revenue — without human orchestration.
</Info>

Superpower does not enhance productivity. It enables **economic agency**.
