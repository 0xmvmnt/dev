# The Missing Economic Layer

The internet monetized attention. Blockchains monetized trust. DeFi monetized liquidity.

**But no system has yet monetized intelligence directly.**

Agents today can detect inefficiencies in capital allocation. They can identify narrative divergence before markets react. They can evaluate probability with computational precision. And yet, when they discover opportunity, they require human permission to act on it.

**Capital without intelligence is inefficient.**
</Note>
